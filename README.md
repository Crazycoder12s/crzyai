# crzyai
a markov ai that learns, its currently blank, it can also draw and edit images
the drawing and editing images part is completely randomised.

How to teach the ai:
If you say [word1] [word2]
The ai will remember word 2 as a word that is associated with word 1
If you say [word1] [word3]
then if you say [word1] it will say either [word2] or [word3]
the more you teach it the better it gets. 
At first its blank though and will most likely just reply with the same phrase of what you said
